Assassin's Creed: Recollection

Assassin's Creed: Recollection is a 2011 iOS app that includes a card game called "Tactics"

Memories

"Memories" is the name given to the cards in this game. Memories are earned by purchasing packs in the in-app store with Animus Credits or as prizes after a battle. There are three different types of memories: Agents, Sites and Actions. The player is granted one free pack of Memories upon starting Tactics.Memories are purchased within a battle using gold. The cost of each memory is relative to its power. Some memories add to the player's daily income once resolved.Memories are divided into six color-coded specialties that reflect the nature of the individuals, actions, and sites within the series: Gold (yellow), Crime (black), Faith (red), Media (purple), Order (blue), and Scholar (green).
